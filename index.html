  • Their Story ❤

    We first met on Oct 10, 2005. I visited her house in Bijapur with my Dad and they had a huge family waiting for us. We met everyone but I don't remember any of that. We had breakfast which was made by Sonal and I was scanning the house to get a glimpse of her and saw her peeking from the Kitchen to do the same. After breakfast, I was told to wait in the room upstairs, sonal came up and sat next to me and we had a chat about our life. We continued chatting regarding Architecture, Engineering, about our common interest etc. We then went to Gol Gumbaz along with Sonal's other cousins where Sonal showed me the salient features of the monument. While there, an interesting thing happened which I think was a message from god. When we were walking out of Gol Gumbaz, an old lady was walking past us looked at us and said "Jodi Channagide" which means "Nice Couple". We then went home and Sonal went into her room with all her family and they discussed for a long time and then her father came out and said they approve of me and they are going to marry thier daughter to me. It was the happiest day of my life till then.
